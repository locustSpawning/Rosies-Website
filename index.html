Since 1961

For over 50 years pleasing you has been our business!

Rosie’s Farm Market is a family owned and operated business committed to providing fresh Jersey produce to the Tri-State area. You can always expect service with a smile and the finest fresh fruit and veggies at the lowest prices.

Conveniently located on Rt. 322 in Mullica Hill, Rosie’s is a one of a kind stop en route to Jersey’s shore points.

Mind • Body • Soul

We’d like to take a moment and thank you for your interest in buying local.

Over the past few years we have seen a sincere excitement towards ‘going green’ and ‘sustainability’ and we are just tickled pink about it!

What we eat is so important to maintaining a happy heart and body. Locally grown produce tastes better. It’s fresh, it’s cheap, and it supports your community, farmers and economy.
